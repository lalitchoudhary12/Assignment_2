Meet Dr. Dhanashree Dalal Simant

Dr. Dhanashree Dalal Simant is the heart and soul of Dentistree Multispeciality Dental Clinic. With a Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) and a Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Pediatric Dentistry, she brings a wealth of expertise and gentle care to her patients.

Her specialization in treating children ensures a compassionate, painless, and fun dental experience, especially for first-time visitors. She is passionate about preventive care and educating both children and parents for better oral hygiene habits.

Dr. Dhanashree believes in the power of empathy and modern dental technology. Her warm nature and child-friendly approach make every visit a comfortable one — helping little smiles grow confidently and healthily.

“A child's smile is the purest form of joy. I'm committed to preserving it with care and compassion.”
